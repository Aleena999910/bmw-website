The Acrylic is a transparent plastic material with exceptional strength, stiffness and optical clarity. Acrylic sheets are easy to make, bond well with adhesives and solvents, and are easy to thermoform.

 

As the name suggests, an acrylic sandwich display frame is made of two sheets of clear acrylic. The actual communication/poster to be displayed is placed between these two sheets. These sheets, since they are made of clear acrylic, give a great glass-like look, minus the weight or handling hassles of glass.
An acrylic bevel edge frame is a type of picture frame made with clear acrylic material. The edges of the frame are cut at an angle to create a beveled effect. Acrylic bevel edge frames offer several benefits, including: Lightweight and durable: Acrylic frames are lightweight, making them easy to handle and hang.
